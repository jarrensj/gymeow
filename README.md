# gymeow

A fitness accountability app that lets you track workouts with your gym community.

## What is gymeow?

gymeow helps fitness communities stay accountable together. Create or join an organization, log your workouts, and see what everyone else is doing.

### The Problem

Staying consistent with workouts is hard. It's easy to skip a day when no one's watching.

### The Solution

gymeow makes your workouts visible to your community. When your gym buddies can see whether you logged a workout today, you're more likely to show up.

## Core Features

- **Organizations**: Create or join a group using a unique slug (e.g., `omakasemoney`, `sushi-run-club`)
- **Workout Logging**: Log workouts in the app or by texting to a phone number
- **Community Feed**: See everyone's workout logs for any given day
- **Calendar View**: Visual calendar showing workout activity across your org
- **Unique Usernames**: Every user has a unique username for their profile

> **Privacy Note (v1.0.0-beta):** Currently, anyone can view any organization's feed and any user's workout logs.
